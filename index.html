Sales Report

The Sales Report summarizes Total Sales, Orders, Average Order Value, and Conversion Rate. It breaks down sales by product, region, and channel, highlights Customer Insights, Top Promotions, Refunds, and tracks Sales Trends and Inventory.

Order Manage

Order Management handles Order Tracking, Status Updates, and Bulk Actions. It includes Order Search/Filters, manages Refunds/Returns, integrates with shipping, and sends notifications for new orders and status changes.

Inventory Manage

Inventory Management tracks stock levels, manages Restocking, and identifies Low Stock Items. It supports Bulk Updates, monitors Inventory Turnover, integrates with suppliers, and provides alerts for stock replenishment.

FAQs

What is Multipurc?

Multipurc is an intuitive eCommerce website builder platform that allows users to create, design, and manage their online stores with ease. Our platform provides a variety of customizable templates, drag-and-drop features, and integrated tools to help you build a professional and functional online store.

Do I need coding skills to use multipurc ?

No, Multipurc is designed for users of all skill levels. Our drag-and-drop interface and pre-built templates make it easy to create a stunning online store without any coding knowledge. Advanced users can also add custom code if desired.

What features does Multipurc offer?

Drag-and-drop website builder & Mobile-responsive designs & Integrated payment gateways & Inventory management & SEO tools & Marketing and promotional tools & Customer support and chat integration & Analytics and reporting

Can I integrate third-party apps with Multipurc?

Yes, Multipurc supports integration with various third-party apps and services, including payment processors, shipping providers, email marketing tools, and more. You can connect these services through our app marketplace or by using API integrations.

How secure is my online store on multipurc?

TWe prioritize security and employ advanced measures to protect your online store and customer data. Our platform uses SSL encryption, regular security updates, and secure payment processing to ensure the safety of your transactions and personal information.
